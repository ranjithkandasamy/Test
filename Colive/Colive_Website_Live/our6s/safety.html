Safetyfirst
Colive cares and provides the maximum security to ensure the safety for each & every residents at the property.
Facial Recognition Access Management Engine
  • Colive has a Frame (Facial Recognition Access Management Engine) across all the properties to shield its residents from non-residents, it protects from or unlikely to cause danger, risk incident
Emergency Response Team
  • ERT (Emergency Response Team) is available 24/7 to its residents in case of any emergency; the team shall response immediately to any incident raised at the doorstep.
CCTV & Video Surveillance
  • Colive properties are well-equipped with CCTV & Video Surveillance. The CSM team (Central Security Monitoring Team) will be monitoring all the events happening across the locations and report any untoward incidents to the security to act on the situation immediately.
