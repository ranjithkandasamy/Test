About Colive

What is Colive?

Colive is a technology powered network of fully-managed ready-to move in homes conveniently located in close proximity to major IT Parks & business hubs. All homes are fully serviced & professionally managed while offering flexible & affordable options suitable for urban living. Experience an upgraded lifestyle with designer rooms, contemporary interiors & thoughtfully designed common spaces with premium amenities and tech enabled security features.

in which cities can I Colive?

Colive is currently operating in 3 metro cities - Bangalore, Chennai & Hyderabad.

How is Colive different?

Co-living is a modern form of housing where residents share common interests & values in a shared economy. Colive Community comprises of a group of distinct millenials who are warm, embracing and inclusive that value openness & collaboration.

Colive make 6 promises to all its residents

  • Style - Designer fully-furnished rooms with chic & contemporary interiors and sprawling common spaces
  • Safety - Tech enabled safety features and Emergency Response Team
  • Social - Community of like-minded millennial
  • Smart - High speed internet, premium amenities & services all through the Colive App
  • Savings - Low deposits, all-inclusive bill and great savings
  • Service - Fully-managed homes with repairs & maintenance support

In interest of the community, residents are advised against having pets in the property. Colive does not object to any pets that could be confined in your room, (Eg- fish) but the staff / community member will not take responsibility for the upkeep or maintenance in your absence.

Security Deposit

How much is the security deposit at Colive?

Unlike the rental industry in Bangalore, Chennai and Hyderabad, the security deposit is equal to just 2 months rent.

Why do I have to pay the security deposit?

The security deposit is a deposit of money to the landlord to ensure that rent will be paid and other responsibilities of the lease performed. Security deposit is collected at the time of check- in solely for security reasons. It will be refunded on 15th of the subsequent month post check- out.

When should the security deposit be paid?

At Colive, you can pay the security deposit in two instalments. One 48 hours prior to the move in and the rest in the subsequent month of your stay.

What payment options are available?

For any difficulty faced while making the transactio, request for assistance. Speak with our customer executive on 7676 000 500 or raise a service request on the website / colive app.

What is the meaning of Separation charges?

Seperation charges are the standard deductions from the security deposit towards the upkeep of the property and maintanence charges that are essential after the room is vacated and before released to a new resident of the Colive Community.

How much is the Separation charges?

Seperation charges will be equivalent to 7 days rent.

What are the deductions in my security deposit?

There will be 7 days rent deduction as seperation charges + 2 months E-bill &power back up + access card + keys + Gas Charges(if any due) + Rent Outstanding(if any due) + RM Charges (if any damages in the room).

Lock-in period

What does the lock-in period mean?

Lock-in period means that the tenant is bound to stay for the minimum agreed period as per policy.

What is the lock-in period for colive properties?

The lock-in period varies from property to property. It starts from 3 months and goes upto 6 months. One can view the lock-in period of the property on the property description page and also in the final statement before payment.

When does my lock-in period begin?

Lock-in period will begin from the move-in date into the property.

What happens if i check-out in my lock-in period?

If the tenant moves-out of the property before the lock-in is completed there will be lock-in period charges of one month rent deduction from the security deposit.

Rent

When is the rent due?

The rent is due every 1st of the calendar month. A late payment charges of rs.500 shall be levied after 7thuntil the 15th. An additional penalty charge equivalent to 7 days rent shall be levied between 16th& 22ndof the month. Wi-fi services will be disconnected and legal notice will be sent to the customer for rent default. In case, rent remains outstanding till 22nd, eviction policies shall apply. Notice will be issued and an additional eviction fee equivalent to 21 days rent will be added to the dues. Access to premises shall be restricted from 23rdand all pending dues and penalties shall be adjusted with the security deposit.

How can I make the payment of my rent?

Rent can be paid through app, website using net banking, upi transfers, debit & credit cards, mobile wallet. The company doesn’t accept any rent or deposit through cash or cheques. Members are requested to strictly use the app or web or directly to colive account for all payments. Any such payments made to anyone claiming to be employees or associates of the company, shall not bind the company or its management.

Where can I find the rent amount & break-up?

What is the rent inclusive of?

The rent includes, Residential rent for the room+serice charges+GST on service charges+Electricity bill(actuals). Apart from these, repair charges(if availed any)+ GST on repair charges+ Gas charges(if availed).

How am i charged for electricity?

Check-in

What does check-in date mean?

Check in date is the date on which a resident moves in the property. Check-in timings are between 12 noon & 9 pm on all days, except Mondays. Luggage cannot be left in the premises prior to check-in. Stay without clearing of deposit or pro-rata rent shall tantamount to illegal stay.

When do I have to check-in?

Check-in has to be done within 15 days of reservation. Failing to do so would lead to the booking being released for sales.

What are the check-in formalities?

Check-in timings are between 12 noon & 9 pm on all days, except Mondays with prior appointment to be fixed with. Luggage cannot be left in the premises prior to check-in. The keys from the central office will only be released once all the check-in formalities including the payment of security deposit & pro-rata rent have been completed. At the time of check-in, members are requested to ensure the following are in order,

  • Clean linen & mattress
  • TV with remote in working condition (if included)
  • Wi-Fi credentials (in case of Wi-Fi dabba, the same shall be activated within 3 working days post check-in)
  • Clean bathrooms with functional geyser, taps, flush & faucets
  • Furniture, furnishings & electronic items in working condition
  • Wardrobes with keys
  • Room key and the main door key
  • Facial recognition or access control cards
  • Dustbin

What documents are required at the time of check-in?

Even though you don’t require any physical documents at the time of check-in, KYC has to be completed beforehand. Non completion of KYC details shall be treated as illegal stay.

Notice period

When can I raise Notice to Vacate?

Notice to Vacate can be raised on or before the 1 st and no later than 3 rd of the calendar month. Ex: If the resident wishes to check out on 31st January, Notice to Vacate has to be raised on or before 1st January and no later than 3rd January (2 days grace period).

How do I raise a Notice to Vacate?

Notice to Vacate should be raised on the Colive App on the “My Bookings” section or on web. A Notice to Vacate communicated to any Colive staff verbally or via service requests and e- mail will not be valid and considered as a Notice to Vacate.

What is the duration of notice period at Colive?

The duration of notice period in Colive is one month which is included is inclusive of the lock-in period as well.

Can I cancel my check-out-out notice?

Yes. If you wish to cancel the Notice to Vacate or change the check-out date, it can be done only within the first 10 days of raising the request.

Can I pre-pone my check-out request?

Yes. If you wish to pre-pone the check-out date, it can be done only within the first 10 days of raising the request. The same can be done under the “My bookings” section on the Colive app or on the website.

Can I postpone my move-out date?

While we would love to have you continue or extend your stay with Colive, our properties are usually in high demand hence postponing your move-out date would be subject to the availability of your booking. If your booking is available, then you can continue staying in the same room. If your room has been booked by another customer, then you will have to move to the room that is allotted by the system after the check-out date. Pro-rata rent for the additional days of your stay will have to be paid prior to the check-out.

What are the charges if the notice period is not served?

In cases where a resident checks-out without serving his/her notice period, 50% of the security deposit will be forfeited during the time of refund.
