Tenant Deposit Agreement and Conditions

This Tenant Deposit AGREEMENT made on the date and at the place stated in the Schedule hereto WHEREAS

a). The Tenant has availed the Company for a Deposit for accommodation requirements.

b). The Company has agreed to grant the Deposit applied for upon the mutually agreed terms and conditions herein appearing. NOW THIS AGREEMENT WITNESSES as under:

Terms

1. Upon the representation and information provided by the Tenant in the application for the Deposit made to the company, the Company hereby agrees to fund to the Tenant the sum specified in the Schedule hereto for the personal requirements of the Tenant, which Deposit is hereinafter referred to as the "Deposit funding".

2. This Agreement and the relevant Schedule as applicable and signed by the Tenant and the Company shall form one single Agreement and shall be read in conjunction with each other.

3. This Agreement shall come to an end upon the Company having, to its satisfaction, received all dues and outstanding, and payments, required to be made by the Tenant hereunder to the company.

4. The Company shall have the right to recall the Deposit at any time at its discretion, without assigning any reasons for such recall, and upon such recall, the Deposit and all other amounts stipulated by the Company shall be payable forthwith. Below are terms agreed by the tenant.

a). Unpaid deposit shall be paid in 5 EMI.

b). Tenant lock in period of minimum 6 months.

c). 100% of deposit shall be forfeited on vacating before minimum lock in period ends.

d). EMI shall be auto debited on the 7th of calendar month to the Companyaccount you have provided.

e). On failure of auto debit of the EMI, there will be a penalty of Rs.500 per transaction.

f). There is no foreclosure of the funded deposit.
